Title page

Add your name, school, date, title, etc. on the custom properties of the document and start working right away. The main title will appear on top of every page. You may even use this data on your document.

Table of contents

The table of contents will build itself from the custom APA style headers. No need to keep track of the page number yourself. You can alter the entire structure of the document and you only need to refresh the table of contents to see the changes.

Bibliography

Create a bibliography database and use it to keep your APA style references in order. It's more work at the beginning but it pays in the long run, specially when you have a long document with hundreds of references.

Creating a title page is simple, just add data to the custom fields and see how it works for you. You may use the custom fields on your document. The table of contents will create itself if you use the custom APA style headers.

Creating a bibliography database is helpful for future reference and will bring you easy acces to such sources in the future. just like the table of contents, you only need to refresh the bibliography to see the changes.
